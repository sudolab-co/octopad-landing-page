FAQ

The common ones.

Do you charge per AI call?

No. You bring your own model keys (Anthropic, OpenAI, etc.) and pay them directly for inference. Octopad charges per seat.

Which AIs does Octopad work with?

Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, and most open models via API. Integrations run locally through your own keys — we never see the payload.

Can I self-host?

Yes, on the Enterprise plan. Octopad ships as a single container plus Postgres. You keep everything on your infra.

What happens to the shared brain if we cancel?

Export it at any time as Markdown or JSON. Starter and Team plans can export with one click; Enterprise gets scheduled exports.

Is there a free trial on the Team plan?

14 days, no card required. After that it's $18 per seat, per month, billed monthly or annually.
