FAQ

The common ones.

Do you charge per AI call?

No. You bring your own model keys (Anthropic, OpenAI, etc.) and pay them directly for inference. Octopad charges per seat.

Which AIs does Octopad work with?

Any AI that supports MCP. That includes Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, and the Gemini CLI today, with more adding MCP support regularly.

What happens if I cancel?

Export your workspace at any time as Markdown or JSON. Your data stays yours.

Is there a free trial on Pro?

Your first month of Pro is free on every new account, no card required. After that it's $9 per seat per month, or $7 billed annually.

The free tier is more than enough to decide. Start a workspace, connect your AI, and run one plan — you'll know within a day.
