Octopad started because every team we knew was running AI on an island — great tools, no shared memory. We're building the back-office those teams wish they had.

Our story

From scattered to shared.

In early 2025, four of us were working on the same product from different timezones — and running three different AIs between us. Each session started from scratch. Every decision got rediscovered.

We built the first version of Octopad for ourselves: a place where any AI could read the same notes, run the same plans, and hand off cleanly between sessions. By the end of the month, our team shipped twice as much. Friends asked for access.

That's what we're building now — not another AI, but the connective tissue between the ones you already use.

What we believe

Three things we keep coming back to.

01

Bring your own AI.

The best model changes every month. Octopad is model-agnostic on purpose — pick whichever is winning this week.

02

Your data stays yours.

We never see your inference payloads. Workspaces export in one click. Self-host whenever you're ready.

03

Boring beats clever.

AI apps should feel as calm as a well-kept notebook. If it surprises you in a bad way, we got it wrong.
