About

We build for
teams who ship.

Octopad started because every team we knew was running AI on an island — great tools, no shared memory. We're building the back-office those teams wish they had.

Our vision

Humans and AI, working together.

The next decade of work is not about replacing humans with agents. It's about humans and AI sharing the same workspace, the same context, and the same source of truth, so each does what it does best.

The honest truth is that AI today is not strong enough to do impactful work on its own. Teams betting on full autonomy keep getting burned. What actually works is symbiosis.

You set direction and make the calls. Your AI handles the structured work: pulling context, drafting plans, capturing decisions, keeping state current. Both of you contribute to the same shared brain.

What we believe

Three things we keep coming back to.

01

Bring your own AI.

The best model changes every month. Octopad is model-agnostic on purpose — pick whichever is winning this week.

02

Your data stays yours.

We never see your inference payloads. Workspaces export in one click. Your workspace content is never used to train AI models.

03

Boring beats clever.

AI apps should feel as calm as a well-kept notebook. If it surprises you in a bad way, we got it wrong.

Where we're going

What's next.

Right now, our focus is nailing the fundamentals of the AI back-office. Context that flows, plans that hold across sessions, a shared brain that stays current as work happens.

Next, we're integrating autonomous agents into the middle of the system — noticing slipping deadlines, flagging stale decisions, keeping plans current without a human pushing every button.

We're also experimenting with agent delegation: you brainstorm a full plan with an AI, then hand the individual steps off to specialized agents. Each one picks up with the back-office context already loaded, runs its part, and reports back into the same shared brain.
