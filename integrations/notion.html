Privacy & security

Your Notion data stays yours

The integration is built with minimal access and full transparency about exactly what we do with your data.

🔒

Read-only access

Octopad never writes to or modifies your Notion workspace. We only read the content you choose to import.

🔑

OAuth 2.0

Secure authorization with automatic token refresh. You authenticate directly with Notion — Octopad never handles your Notion password.

✅

You choose what to import

You select exactly which pages and databases to import. Nothing is imported without explicit selection — no surprise bulk imports.

⚡

Rate-limited and batched

Imports are processed in batches of up to 50 items at a time to respect Notion's API rate limits and ensure reliability.
